Juseong Park

Ph.D. Student | Wireless Communications | Machine Learning

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Jeffrey Andrews. Currently, I am a Graduate Research Assistant with the Wireless Networking and Communication Group (WNCG) and the 6G@UT Research Center. My research primarily focuses on the intersection of machine learning and wireless communications.

I received my B.E. and M.E. degrees in electrical engineering from Korea University, where I was advised by Prof. Inkyu Lee during my master’s studies. I have previously held internships at Nokia Bell Labs in Murray Hill, NJ; Nokia Standards in Naperville, IL; and Qualcomm in San Diego, CA.
