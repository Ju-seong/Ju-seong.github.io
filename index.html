Juseong Park

Ph.D. Student | Wireless Communications | Machine Learning

Juseong Park received the B.E. and M.E. degrees in electrical engineering from Korea University, Seoul, South Korea. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in electrical and computer engineering with the University of Texas at Austin, Austin, TX, USA. He is currently a Graduate Research Assistant with the Wireless Networking and Communication Group (WNCG) and the 6G@UT Research Center, UT Austin. He has previously held internships at Nokia Bell Labs, Murray Hill, NJ, USA; and Nokia Standards, Naperville, IL, USA. His research interests lie mainly in the intersection of machine learning and wireless networks.
