Juseong Park

Ph.D. Candidate @ UT Austin | Wireless Communications & ML Researcher

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin, advised by Prof. Jeffrey Andrews. As a Graduate Research Assistant with the Wireless Networking and Communication Group (WNCG) and the 6G@UT Research Center, my research focuses on the intersection of machine learning (Diffusion Models, Deep Learning) and wireless communications (MIMO, CSI Feedback).

Previously, I received my B.E. and M.E. degrees from Korea University, advised by Prof. Inkyu Lee. I have gained industrial research experience through internships at Nokia Bell Labs, Nokia Standards, and Qualcomm.

Research Interests

  • Wireless Communications: MU-MIMO, CSI Feedback, Beam Tracking
  • Machine Learning: Generative Models (Diffusion), Deep Reinforcement Learning
  • Signal Processing: Compressive Sensing, Optimization
